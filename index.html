About Me

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and went to college at UC Berkeley where I studied Political Economy. However, I had always been interested in tech so I decided to work at Quixey as a QA Tester, which piqued my interest in software development. Working in QA at Quixey was great but I didn't want to just build automation tools. Instead I wanted to build tools of greater scale. Tools that I can see have a positive effect in people's lives. The two most satisfying feelings of being a developer is finally being able to solve the problem you have been stuck on for hours and seeing others find utility in something you've created.
